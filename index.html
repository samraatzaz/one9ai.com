Frequently Asked Questions

Everything You Need
To Know.

Will patients or clients know they're talking to an AI?
Sara is designed to sound warm, professional, and natural. Many callers will not immediately realize they're speaking with an AI. That said, Sara discloses at the start of every call that the call may be recorded for quality assurance — which is our standard transparency practice. We can also configure Sara to identify herself as an AI assistant if your business or jurisdiction requires it.
Can we keep our existing phone number?
Yes. In most cases, your current business phone number can be forwarded to Sara's line or ported to our system. Your patients and clients dial the same number they always have — they'll just notice that someone always answers now.
How long does setup take?
Most businesses go live within 7 days of our first call. We handle the entire setup — voice configuration, system prompt, calendar integration, testing, and go-live. You don't need to learn any new software or change how your team operates.
What happens if Sara can't handle a call?
We configure a transfer protocol for every client. If a caller has an emergency, asks a question outside Sara's scope, or simply requests to speak with a human, Sara transfers the call to your designated team member — with a brief verbal summary of the conversation so far.
Does Sara integrate with Google Calendar?
Yes. Sara connects directly to your Google Calendar and checks real-time availability before booking any appointment. This prevents double bookings and ensures your calendar is always accurate. We handle the entire integration during setup.
What's included in the setup fee?
The setup fee covers Sara's complete configuration for your business — including your script, intake questions, doctor or staff schedules, calendar integration, SMS configuration, testing across multiple call scenarios, and your go-live support. Once you're live, the ongoing monthly fee covers continued operation, monitoring, and direct support from our team.
Is there a long-term contract?
No. Monthly service fees are billed month-to-month. You can cancel with 7 days' written notice at any time. We believe the service should earn your business every month — not lock you in.
Do you serve businesses outside of Calgary?
Yes. One9 AI serves businesses across Canada, the United States, and internationally. Our setup process is entirely remote — no on-site visit required. We serve clients in multiple time zones and can work with any North American or international phone system.
How is pricing determined?
Setup fees start at $399 USD and monthly service fees start at $599 USD. Final pricing is customized based on your call volume, number of locations, calendar integrations, SMS usage, and any custom workflows. We provide an exact quote in writing after a short discovery call. No hidden fees.
